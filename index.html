About Me

I received my undergraduate degree in Computer Science and Technology from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT), where I am currently pursuing my master's degree. My research interests include microgesture VR interaction, pose-guided hand image generation, human-like teleoperation, and dexterous manipulation.
